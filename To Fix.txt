1. Neworld's AI uses Ollama — a free, fully local AI that runs entirely on your machine. No API keys, no internet required.

Setup steps:

Download and install Ollama from ollama.com
Open a terminal and run:
ollama pull llama3
Also pull an embedding model:
ollama pull nomic-embed-text
Make sure Ollama is running (it starts automatically on most systems after install)
Once Ollama is running, the AI panel will let you ask questions about your world, get writing suggestions, and check story consistency — all powered by your local models.
